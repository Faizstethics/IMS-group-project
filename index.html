Activities

Team Mega Ceria Management has made a charitable visit to Laila Taib Welfare Complex, Peryatim Kuching, on August 19, 2023, at the KKLT Multipurpose Hall on Saturday. The visit included not only friendly interactions and donations but also a variety of activities. The programs organized during this visit included a painting and coloring competition, a group slogan creation contest, mini-games, and a dance session. These initiatives aimed to bring joy to the children and provide a memorable experience through engaging and creative activities

On November 18, 2023, Sarawak Energy organized a Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) program at Laila Taib Welfare Complex, Peryatim Kuching, encompassing a visit and electrical safety briefing for the children at the complex. This program, involving both CSR activities and a safety talk, was carried out with utmost dedication at the KKLT Multipurpose Hall. Not only did it provide valuable insights into electrical safety, but it also showcased Sarawak Energy's commitment to assisting the community and improving the quality of life for the children from Peryatim .

The children from Peryatim Kuching also have joined the "Effective Goal Achievement" program organized by the Sarawak Social Welfare Council. This program was held on October 21, 2023, from 8:15 am to 1:00 pm at Chemsain, Rafflesia Room, Level 6, Jalan Rock, 93200 Kuching. This program has given these young individuals an opportunity to enhance their self-confidence and perseverance. By actively participating in the program, the children from Peryatim not only acquire valuable skills but also engage in personal development activities that can positively shape their future.
